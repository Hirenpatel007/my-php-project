About the University

Sardar Patel University is one of the renowned and well-established universities in the state of Gujarat, India. It was founded in 1955 under an Act of the Legislative Assembly of the then Bombay State, inspired by the vision of Shri Bhaikaka and Shri Bhikhabhai Saheb, and named in honor of India’s Iron Man, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. The University has been playing a vital role in the educational, cultural, and socio-economic development of the region. In line with academic excellence and integrity, it was accredited with an ‘A’ Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) in 2016. The University has also been recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 12(B), and has received funding under various national schemes including the DST-FIST and DBT-Builder programs. With its motto of “Excellence Matters”, Sardar Patel University continues to uphold the legacy of its namesake through quality education, innovative research, and community engagement.
